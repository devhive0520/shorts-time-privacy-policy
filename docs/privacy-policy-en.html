1. Purpose of Privacy Policy

Shorts Time (hereinafter referred to as "Service") values users' personal information and has established and published this Privacy Policy to safely protect and manage users' personal information in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, including privacy protection laws.

2. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The Service processes minimal information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: Service provision and operation
  • Advertising: Displaying ads to provide free service
  • Subscription Payment Management: Processing in-app purchases and subscription payments

3. Items of Personal Information Processed

The Service collects the following information solely for advertising and app error analysis purposes:

  • Device Information: Device model name, operating system version (non-personally identifiable)

The Service does not collect personally identifiable information such as account creation, login credentials, email addresses, phone numbers, or location information.

4. Period of Processing and Retention of Personal Information

All information is deleted along with the app when the app is uninstalled. The Service does not store personal information on servers, and all information is stored only on the device.

5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Service does not provide personal information to third parties.

However, advertising network providers may collect advertising IDs and device information for ad delivery and performance measurement, which is subject to their respective privacy policies. In-app purchases and subscription payments are processed through app stores (Google Play, App Store), and payment information is managed by those platforms.

6. Entrustment of Personal Information Processing

The Service does not entrust personal information processing.

7. Measures to Ensure Personal Information Security

The Service takes the following measures to ensure personal information security:

  • The app does not transmit personal information over the network and stores it only on the device.
  • Personal information is not stored on servers.
  • Only minimal non-personally identifiable information is collected.

8. Rights and Obligations of Users and How to Exercise Them

All information is deleted when the app is uninstalled. Since the Service does not store personal information on servers, there is no need for separate procedures to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information.

To reset your advertising ID or limit ad tracking, you can adjust the following in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (turn off)
