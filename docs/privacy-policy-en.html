Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

1. Purpose of Privacy Policy

Shorts Time (hereinafter referred to as "Service") values users' personal information and has established and published this Privacy Policy to safely protect and manage users' personal information in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, including the Personal Information Protection Act and the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection.

2. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The Service processes personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service provision and operation
  • User identification and verification
  • Service usage statistics and analysis
  • Customer consultation and inquiry processing
  • Service improvement and new service development
  • Sanctions against violations of laws and terms of service

3. Items of Personal Information Processed

3.1 Required Collection Items

  • Device Information: Device unique identifier, operating system information, app version
  • Service Usage Information: Service usage records, access logs, usage statistics, viewing history
  • Content Information: Videos, images, text, and other content created by users

3.2 Optional Collection Items

  • Profile Information: Nickname, profile picture, bio (when using the service)
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number (for customer support)
  • Social Media Integration Information: Information provided by social media platforms when linking accounts
  • Other Information: Information voluntarily provided by users

4. Period of Processing and Retention of Personal Information

4.1 Processing Period

  • Service Usage Period: While using the service
  • After Account Deletion: Immediately deleted (except when retention is required by law)

4.2 Retention Period

  • Service Usage Records: 3 years (Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, etc. Act)
  • Customer Consultation Records: 3 years (Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, etc. Act)
  • Content Information: Immediately deleted upon account deletion (except when retention is required by law)
  • Other Retention Required by Law: Period specified in relevant laws

5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Service does not provide users' personal information to external parties in principle. However, exceptions are made in the following cases:

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When required by law or when requested by investigative agencies in accordance with procedures and methods prescribed by law for investigative purposes
  • When providing the minimum necessary information for service provision (in such cases, users will be notified in advance)

6. Entrustment of Personal Information Processing

The Service may entrust personal information processing to the following parties for smooth service provision:

  • Cloud Services: Server operation and data storage
  • Analytics Services: Service usage statistics and analysis
  • Customer Support: Customer consultation and inquiry processing
  • Content Delivery Network (CDN): Video and media file delivery

Entrusted companies are managed and supervised to safely process personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act.

7. Measures to Ensure Personal Information Security

The Service takes the following measures to ensure personal information security:

  • Administrative Measures: Establishment of personal information protection policies, regular employee training
  • Technical Measures: Access rights management for personal information processing systems, installation of access control systems, encryption of personal information, installation of security programs
  • Physical Measures: Access control for computer rooms, data storage rooms, etc.

8. Rights and Obligations of Users and How to Exercise Them

Users, as subjects of personal information, may exercise the following rights:

  • Request for notification of personal information processing status
  • Request for access to personal information
  • Request for correction or deletion of personal information
  • Request for suspension of personal information processing

Rights may be exercised through written documents, phone, email, facsimile (FAX), etc., in accordance with Article 41, Paragraph 1 of the Enforcement Decree of the Personal Information Protection Act, and the Service will take action without delay.

9. Processing of Personal Information of Children Under 14

Important: The Service does not collect personal information of children under 14 years of age. If a child under 14 wishes to use the service, consent from a legal guardian is required.

When collecting personal information of children under 14, the Service processes personal information after obtaining consent from legal guardians. Legal guardians may exercise rights such as access, correction, deletion, and suspension of processing of the child's personal information.
