1. Purpose of Privacy Policy

Shorts Time (hereinafter referred to as "Service") values users' privacy and has established and published this Privacy Policy to inform users about how we handle information in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, including privacy protection laws.

2. Collection and Use of Personal Information

Important: Shorts Time does not collect personal information from users. The Service does not collect personally identifiable information such as account creation, login credentials, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other information that can identify individuals.

The Service uses minimal information only for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: Providing app functionality and operation
  • Advertising: Displaying ads to provide free service
  • In-App Purchases: Processing subscription payments and in-app purchases

3. Information Collected

3.1 Automatically Collected Information

The Service may automatically collect the following non-personally identifiable information:

  • Device Information: Device unique identifier (non-personally identifiable), operating system information, app version
  • Service Usage Information: App usage statistics, error logs (non-personally identifiable)

This information cannot be used to identify individuals and is used solely for service improvement purposes.

3.2 Information Not Collected

The Service does not collect the following information:

  • Personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number
  • Location information
  • Personal data on device such as contacts, photos, files
  • Social media account information

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

4.1 Advertising Networks

The Service displays advertisements to provide free service. Advertising network providers may collect the following information for ad delivery and performance measurement:

  • Advertising ID (non-personally identifiable)
  • Device information (operating system, device model, etc.)
  • Advertising interaction information

For detailed information about how advertising networks handle personal information, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

4.2 In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The Service provides in-app purchases and subscription payments. Payments are processed through app stores (Google Play, App Store), and payment information is managed by those platforms.

  • Google Play: Google's Privacy Policy applies.
  • App Store: Apple's Privacy Policy applies.

The Service does not directly collect or store payment information.

5. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

Since the Service does not collect personal information, we do not retain or use personal information. Collected non-personally identifiable information is used solely for service improvement purposes and is retained only when required by law.

6. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

Since the Service does not collect personal information, we do not provide personal information to third parties. However, advertising networks and payment platforms may process information according to their respective privacy policies.

7. User Rights

Since the Service does not collect personal information, there is no need for procedures related to requests for access, correction, or deletion of personal information.

To reset your advertising ID or limit ad tracking, you can adjust the following in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (turn off)
